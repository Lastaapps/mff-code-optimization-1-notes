## Lattice of Values
The diagram shows a flat lattice for constant propagation:
- **Top Element**: (Implied "Undefined" or "Top")
- **Middle Elements**: `0`, `1`, `2`, ... (all constant values)
- **Bottom Element**: `VAR` (also known as "Any" or "Bottom", representing a non-constant value)

The meet operation (∧) behavior:
- `X ∧ X = X` (Idempotency)
- `X ∧ Y = VAR` (if X ≠ Y, the result is non-constant)
