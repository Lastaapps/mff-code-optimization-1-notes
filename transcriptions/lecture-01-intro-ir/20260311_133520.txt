## Data-Flow Analysis Assumptions
To ensure the correctness and termination of the iterative algorithm, the following must hold:

1. **Meet must be Monotone**:
    - If $x \leq x'$ and $y \leq y'$, then $x \wedge y \leq x' \wedge y'$.
2. **Jump functions must be Monotone**:
    - $J_B(x) \leq J_B(x')$

---

## Theorem (THM)
**Iterative dataflow is finite if the semi-lattice has finite height.**
- This guarantees that the algorithm will eventually reach a fixed point.
