## Lazy Code Motion (LCM) Algorithm Steps
The full sequence of dataflow analyses required for LCM:

1. **Calculate Local Properties**: $EUSE$ (upward exposed uses) and $KILL$ (redefinitions).
2. **Anticipancy (Backward)**: Compute where expressions are down-safe.
3. **Availability (Forward)**: Compute where expressions are available.
4. **EARLIEST**: Compute the earliest points where it is safe and beneficial to place an expression.
5. **Postponability (Forward)**: Determine if a computation can be moved later in the CFG without losing the benefits of redundancy elimination.
6. **LATEST**: The final placement points after "laziness" is applied.
7. **Transformation**: Insert computations at $LATEST$ and remove original redundant computations.
