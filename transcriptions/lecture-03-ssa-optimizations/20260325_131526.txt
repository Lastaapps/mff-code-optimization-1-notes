## Lazy Code Motion (LCM)
LCM is an improvement over earlier PRE (Partial Redundancy Elimination) algorithms. It aims to eliminate redundancy while also minimizing register pressure.

### Goals of LCM:
1. **No path contains redundant computation**: Every path from entry to exit should calculate an expression at most once.
2. **No new computation added to any path**: Ensures safety (the program won't crash due to a moved expression).
3. **No computation is moved earlier than required by 1 and 2**: This is the "Lazy" part. By postponing the computation as long as possible, we reduce the live ranges of registers.
