## Critical Edges
**Definition:** An edge $B \to B'$ is called **critical** if:
- Basic Block $B$ has **multiple successors**.
- Basic Block $B'$ has **multiple predecessors**.

Splitting critical edges is a prerequisite for many code motion optimizations (like PRE and LCM) to avoid the "lost update" or "incorrect placement" problems.
