## Anticipancy (Backward Analysis)
$$ANT IN(B) = EUSE(B) \cup (ANT OUT(B) \setminus KILL(B))$$
$$ANT OUT(B) = \bigcap_{S \in SUCC(B)} ANT IN(S)$$

## Busy Code Motion
The goal is to move expressions as far "up" (towards entry) as possible while maintaining safety.
1. **Critical Edge Splitting**: Ensure every edge from a block with multiple successors to a block with multiple predecessors is split.
2. **Moving Expressions to Anticipancy Boundary**: Place the computation at the earliest point where it is anticipated.
