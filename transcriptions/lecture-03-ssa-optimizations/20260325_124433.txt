### Partially Available Expressions
**Forward Analysis ($\cup$)**

$$PAV IN(B) = \bigcap_{P \in PRED(B)} PAV OUT(P)$$

## Partial Redundancy Elimination (PRE)
**Knoop, Rüthing, Steffen (1992)**

**Definition:** Expression $E$ is **partially redundant** if **some** path from entry to $E$ calculates $E$ before.

---

## CFG Diagram Sketch
A diagram shows a CFG with an entry and exit.
- One path contains a calculation of `A+B`.
- Another path joins later where `A+B` is used again.
- If `A+B` is calculated on one path but not the other, it is partially redundant at the join point.
