## Reaching Definitions (RD)

### Equations (Forward Data-Flow)
- **RD IN(b)**:
  $$RD IN(b) = \begin{cases} \emptyset & \text{if } b = \text{ENTRY} \\ \bigcup_{p \in \text{Pred}(b)} RD OUT(p) \end{cases}$$
- **RD OUT(b)**:
  $$RD OUT(b) = RD_{local}(b) \cup (RD IN(b) \setminus \text{kill}(b))$$

### Implementation Details
- **Data Structures**:
    - BITMAP
    - SBITMAP (Small Bitmap)
    - EBITMAP (Extra/Enhanced Bitmap)
    - SPARSE BITMAP (efficient for large, sparse sets)
- **Operations**: Efficient implementation using OR and AND-NOT bitwise operations.
